Project 2

Oroa

Oroa is a lounge chair inspired by natural geometry and the branching logic of neurons.

The form follows soft, continuous curves, creating a calm and inviting presence. It features a wide seat and a gently reclined backrest for a relaxed sitting posture.

H 700mm, W 800mm

Material

The seating is finished in a textured fabric, providing comfort and a warm tactile quality

Project 1

Veya

Veya is a salt shaker inspired by the structure of a neuron

The top represents the cell, while the internal metal frame shows the connections neurons.

Translucent outer layer acts like a skin, enclosing and holding the structure together.

Material

Cap

Natural wood (e.g., Walnut for the darker one, Oak or Beech for the lighter one)

Outer Shell

Inner Shell (Core)

Food-grade stainless steel or anodized aluminum
